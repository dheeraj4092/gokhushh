## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### For Best Quality:
- Keep original videos in 1080x1920 resolution (vertical)
- Use good lighting and audio in recordings
- Film in stable conditions (avoid shaky footage)

### For Smallest File Size:
- Adjust CRF value in script (higher = smaller, try 25-27)
- Record at 720p instead of 1080p if acceptable
- Keep videos under 30 seconds when possible

### For Best Performance:
- Always provide poster thumbnails (auto-generated by script)
- Use consistent naming conventions
- Keep total size of all videos under 30 MB

## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### "FFmpeg not found"
```bash

```

### Videos too large after optimization?
Edit `optimize-videos.sh` and change `-crf 23` to `-crf 25`

### Videos won't play in browser?
Ensure they're:
- MP4 format
- H.264 codec
- Proper path: `/testimonials/videos/...`

### Need different quality?
```bash

-crf 20

# Lower quality (smaller file)
-crf 27
```
